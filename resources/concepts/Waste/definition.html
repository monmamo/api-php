A place that is considered barren, unproductive, and often unsuitable for habitation or agriculture.

A Waste can result from natural processes, such as erosion or volcanic activity, or they can be caused by human activities, such as pollution or deforestation.

Wastelands do not necessarily have to be arid or devoid of water. They can include polluted or contaminated areas, degraded landscapes, or places that have been significantly altered or damaged.

Wastelands may not always have a unique ecosystem or the potential to support life as deserts do. They are often seen as areas of neglect or environmental degradation.
