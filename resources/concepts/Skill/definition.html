An action that a monster takes against any number of other monsters or anthropes.

A Skill is something that a Monster can do only by being taught and learning how to do it.

A Skill produces one or more of the following effects:

  • Causes Damage to one or more Monsters.
  • Prevents Damage from being done to a Monster (usually self).
  • Changes a Monster property or condition for the current Turn or future Turns.
  • Causes a change in the Weather.
  • Enables the Monster to transport the Trainer (see Movement).

A Skill targets a number of Monsters (usually one, perhaps more, sometimes zero) but can potentially affect all Monsters in play.
